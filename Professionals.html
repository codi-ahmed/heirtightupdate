The New Standard for Professionals

Heir Tight redefines how firms, advisors, and institutions organize, share, and safeguard critical information.

  • All-in-One Platform
    Manage every client’s estate, business, and personal records under one secure roof.
  • Bank-Level Protection
    Every file is encrypted with 256-bit AES security—the same technology trusted by financial institutions and government agencies.
  • Client Collaboration Simplified
    Share, request, and approve documents in real time without relying on email or unsecured cloud folders.
  • Compliance Made Easy
    Track every upload, signature, and access point with full audit trails. Maintain regulatory standards effortlessly.
  • White-Labeled for Your Brand
    Offer Heir Tight as your own digital vault, tailored to your logo, colors, and firm identity.
  • Actionable Insights
    Access a professional dashboard that tracks engagement, pending tasks, and renewal dates—helping you serve clients proactively, not reactively.
