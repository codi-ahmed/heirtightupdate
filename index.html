What’s a Family Office?

Traditionally, a family office is a private system wealthy families use to manage their money, documents, and legacy under one roof. It handles everything from investments and taxes to wills, trusts, and philanthropy—keeping the family’s wealth organized and protected for generations.

At Heir Tight, we believe every family deserves that same level of order, protection, and peace of mind—without needing millions in the bank.

That’s why we created the best all-in-one digital vault:

  • Organize your life and legacy in one secure place
  • Protect what matters most with smart tools and reminders
  • Prepare your family for every stage of life and succession

Heir Tight is the digital family office for everyday families—making legacy management simple, secure, and accessible to all.
