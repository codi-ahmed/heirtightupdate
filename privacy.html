Heir Tight™ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/20/2021
Last Updated: 10/20/2025

1. Introduction
Welcome to Heir Tight, LLC (“Heir Tight,” “we,” “our,” or “us”).
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Heir Tight™ platform (“Service” or “Platform”).
By accessing or using Heir Tight, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
We collect only the information necessary to operate, secure, and improve your experience.

a. Information You Provide
• Account Information: Name, email, phone number, and billing details when you register.
• Documents and Uploads: Files, notes, and metadata you voluntarily upload or store in your secure vault.
• Heir Information: Contact information for heirs or designated individuals you choose to add.
• Payment Details: Processed securely through our third-party payment processor; we do not store full credit card information.
• Support Requests: Any correspondence with our support team.

b. Information Collected Automatically
• Log files, IP addresses, and device identifiers to protect your account and improve performance.
• Cookies and similar technologies to enable secure sign-ins and session management.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to provide and improve the Heir Tight experience. Specifically, we use it to:
• Operate and maintain your account and stored vaults.
• Process payments and subscriptions.
• Facilitate heir sharing and document permissions.
• Verify user and heir identities for security purposes.
• Communicate service updates, notifications, or security alerts.
• Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.

We never sell, rent, or trade your information for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security
All files and data are stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 servers using bank-level AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit.
Heir Tight implements multi-layered security, including authentication logs, audit trails, and administrative controls.
Only you and those you authorize can view your uploaded files.
We conduct regular security audits and follow ISO 156–aligned data protection standards.

5. Heir Access and Proof of Death Verification
You may designate specific “Heirs” who can access selected folders or documents.
Files marked as “Hidden” remain inaccessible until the proof of death process is completed.
Verification is handled by a trusted third-party provider who confirms the validity of death certificates or official records before access is granted.
Once verified, heirs receive time-limited credentials to unlock only approved files.
Heir Tight does not release any hidden documents without verified authorization.

6. Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service or comply with legal requirements.
When you delete your account or your subscription lapses:
• Your data remains accessible for 30 days.
• After 30 days, data is permanently deleted from our systems and backups.
• In the event Heir Tight ceases operations, all files will be securely zipped, encrypted, and sent to you before deletion.

7. Your Privacy Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
• Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
• Correction: Request updates or corrections to inaccurate information.
• Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and files.
• Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.
• Restriction/Objection: Limit or object to processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, contact us at legal@getheirtight.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children’s Privacy
Heir Tight is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
If we discover data collected from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately.

9. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We only share data with trusted service providers essential to platform operation. These include:
• Amazon Web Services (AWS): for cloud storage.
• Payment Processors: to manage secure transactions.
• Proof-of-Death Verification Partner: to authenticate heir access.

All partners are bound by strict confidentiality, data security, and processing agreements.
We do not share data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

10. International Users
Heir Tight’s primary data centers are located in the United States.
If you access the platform from outside the U.S., you consent to the transfer and processing of your data under U.S. privacy laws, which may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

11. Your Data Choices
You may update or delete your information at any time through your dashboard.
You may opt out of non-essential emails while still receiving security and transactional notices.
Deleting your account will permanently remove all stored data after 30 days.

12. Data Breach Notification
If a data breach occurs that may affect your personal information, Heir Tight will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the incident, in accordance with applicable law.

13. Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Updates will be posted on our website with a revised effective date.
Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.
