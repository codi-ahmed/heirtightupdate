The first digital family office
for every household

At Heir Tight™, we believe your legacy deserves more than a drawer full of documents — it deserves a living, breathing system that moves with you through life.

We built Heir Tight to protect what matters most — your people, your plans, and your peace of mind. Born out of love, loss, and the realization that too many families are unprepared, Heir Tight transforms the chaos of life’s paperwork into clarity, connection, and confidence.

Our Story

When our founder experienced firsthand how difficult it was for families to find, manage, and communicate critical information during life’s most vulnerable moments, she knew something had to change.

Out of that pain came purpose — a platform that ensures no one ever says, “I didn’t know,” again.

  • What began as a secure digital vault has evolved into a full ecosystem — combining technology, trust, and human connection. Today, Heir Tight helps families, professionals, and entire communities organize, protect, and share essential life, legal, and business documents — safely, intelligently, and beautifully.
Why We Exist

Most people don’t know this, but ultra-wealthy families have what’s called a “Family Office.” These private organizations manage everything — from estate planning, insurance, and investments, to tax strategy, philanthropy, and family governance.

But we believe every family deserves that same level of protection, structure, and peace of mind — without needing millions in the bank.

That’s why we created Heir Tight™ — the digital family office for everyday families.
We’ve built the tools that make it possible to:

  • Securely store and share life, legal, and business documents
  • Manage heirs, executors, and beneficiaries in one place
  • Automate reminders for key renewals and updates
  • Communicate plans clearly with family and professionals
  • Access AI-powered guidance and estate creation tools
Because legacy shouldn’t just belong to the wealthy
it should belong to all of us.

OURMISSION

To make generational wealth, family communication, and legacy management simple, secure, and accessible for everyone.

We’re on a mission to close the $68 billion gap left behind each year from unclaimed assets and unprepared estates — because we believe every family deserves to be seen, safe, and prepared.

OURFUTURE

With innovations like Heiryn™ your proactive AI Legacy Assistant, and HeirTag™, our NFC-powered connection system, Heir Tight is redefining what it means to live organized and leave a legacy.

We’re not just protecting documents — we’re building digital family offices for everyday people.

Together, we’re creating a world where every life is honored, every legacy is secured, and every family stays connected — tightly.
